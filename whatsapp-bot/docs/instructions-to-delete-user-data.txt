Instructions to Delete User Data

To request the deletion of your personal data and chat history from the Harvest Schools WhatsApp Bot, please email:

inquiries@harvestschools.com

Please include the following information in your email to help us process your request efficiently:
1. Your WhatsApp phone number associated with the bot.
2. Full name (if applicable).
3. A clear statement requesting the deletion of your personal data and chat history.

Once we receive your request, we will verify the information provided and proceed with the deletion of your data from our systems. Please allow up to 30 days for us to complete this process.

We will confirm via email once your data has been deleted.
