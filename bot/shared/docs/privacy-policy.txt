Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
Welcome to the Harvest Schools WhatsApp Bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our automated WhatsApp assistant.

2. Information We Collect
When you use our WhatsApp bot, we collect and store the following information in our database:
* Contact Information: Your WhatsApp phone number.
* Chat History: The messages you send to the bot and the bot's responses.
* Session Data: Your language preferences, current session state, and timestamps of your interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information
The data we collect is used strictly to operate and improve the bot's functionality, including:
* Providing relevant and context-aware responses to your inquiries.
* Maintaining chat history to support continuous conversations.
* Routing your inquiries to the correct school department when necessary.

4. Third-Party Data Processing
To provide intelligent responses, your messages are processed by third-party services. By using the bot, you consent to your data being shared securely with:
* Meta (WhatsApp API): For the transmission and delivery of messages.
* AI/LLM Providers: Your chat history and prompts are sent to our AI partners (such as Google Gemini or Deepseek) strictly for generating conversational responses.

5. Data Retention and Deletion
We store your chat history and session data to improve your experience over time. 
To request the deletion of your personal data and chat history, please email inquiries@harvestschools.com.
